Welcome to Blogochain!

How to get started:

  1. Enter a transaction: Type any text (like "Alice sent 10 coins to Bob" or "Coffee purchase $5") in the transaction field below
  2. Add it to the blockchain: Click the "Add Transaction" button to add your transaction to the pending pool
  3. Mine a block: Click "Mine Block" to permanently record your transactions in the blockchain
  4. Explore: Watch your blockchain grow and search for specific transactions!

Tip: Try entering transactions like "Payment for coffee", "Salary deposit", or any message you want to store permanently!
